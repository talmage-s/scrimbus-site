About the Designer

I am currently studying at Utah Valley University. My practice is driven by a deep appreciation for structured visual systems, from the rigorous grids of modernism to the rich, narrative context found throughout art history.

I firmly believe that effective communication is the foundation of all good design.

My Four-Step Design Process

  1. Research and Discovery: Understanding the client and target audience.
  2. Conceptualization: Sketching and brainstorming initial ideas.
  3. Refinement: Moving to digital tools like Illustrator and refining the layout.
  4. Delivery: Finalizing files, dielines, and style guides for the client.

Key Design Terminology

Kerning
The adjustment of space between individual characters in typography.
Dieline
A template used in packaging design to ensure proper layout, folding, and cutting.
Hierarchy
The visual arrangement of elements in a way that implies importance.
