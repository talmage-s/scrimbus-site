The Designer

Hello, I'm Talmage. I am a graphic design student currently studying at Utah Valley University. My practice is driven by a deep appreciation for structured visual systems, from the rigorous grids of modernism to the rich, narrative context found throughout art history.

I believe that effective design exists at the intersection of aesthetic beauty and functional communication. Whether I am crafting a physical packaging dieline, pairing functional sans-serifs like Bahnschrift, or developing the visual identity for an indie record label, my goal is always to create an immersive, cohesive brand experience.

Core Competencies

Brand Identity
Developing comprehensive visual systems, including logos, color palettes, and brand voice guidelines for studios and commercial spaces.
Typography
Macro and micro typographic detailing, focusing on legibility, kerning, and establishing clear visual hierarchies.
Print & Packaging
Translating digital concepts into the physical realm through structural dielines, physical mockups, and layout design.
