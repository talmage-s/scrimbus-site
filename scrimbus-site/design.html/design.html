The 4 Pillars of Design

When creating a layout, keep these four concepts in mind to ensure clarity and visual appeal.

  • Contrast: Making distinct elements stand out.
  • Repetition: Using consistent styles to create unity.
  • Alignment: Placing items so they have a visual connection.
  • Proximity: Grouping related items together.
