Welcome to Better Design

This site explores the fundamental concepts of graphic design, including typography, color theory, and layout. Understanding how elements interact on a page is crucial for effective visual communication.

Whether you are designing a digital interface or a physical poster, the way you guide the viewer's eye determines the success of your work. Good design is not just about making things look good; it is about conveying a message clearly and instantly.

Key Terms

Kerning
The spacing between individual characters in a word.
Leading
The vertical distance between lines of text.
Hex Code
A six-digit code used to represent colors on the web.
